Project Overview

Adinkrahene is an initiative of the Foundation which allows a customer to accumulate points on all services and products of Vodafone, which would be redeemable for health benefits. It is intended to create a health insurance package for all subscribers of the programme. As the Foundation strives to maintain the lead in health care in the country, it is our policy to provide sustainable and affordable access to health care to all persons. Adinkra comes as a response of the Foundation's quest to add value to services used by customers, especially in the field of health. All customers will have the opportunity to subscribe to the service. This process will help generate a unique identification code for the customer which will allow him/her to have access to any of the health facilities and vendors enlisted on the Vodafone Adinkra Health Network for medical services.
