About Foundation

Vodafone Ghana Foundation was established in 2009 as the charitable arm of Vodafone Ghana to improve the livelihood as well as address some socio-economic problems of underprivileged population in Ghana. The Foundation has over the years participated immensely in community development, as part of its strategic plan, to become the leader in social intervention campaigns in the area of health, education and digital. As it was previously determined, there are three main CSR areas of the Foundation; Health, Education and Digital. With the increasing burden of diseases across the country, the deterioration in the human capital development in Ghana, it is imperative for a change in the approach towards social interventions of cooperate organisations to drive a positive community building agenda.
